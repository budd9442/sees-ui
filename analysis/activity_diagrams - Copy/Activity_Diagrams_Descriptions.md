 Activity Diagrams for Existing System

 2.5.1 Activity diagram for Register Modules

Activity diagram explains how a student registers for academic modules using physical forms in the existing system. The process involves manual form filling, submission to the academic office, and manual prerequisite checking by office staff.

 2.5.2 Activity diagram for Select Degree Program

Activity diagram explains how students select their degree program (MIT or IT) through the eKel Learning Management System at the beginning of their 2nd year. The process involves logging into eKel, reviewing available programs, making a selection, and receiving confirmation from the system.

 2.5.3 Activity diagram for Select Specialization

Activity diagram explains how MIT students select their specialization (BSE, OSCM, or IS) through the eKel Learning Management System at the beginning of their 3rd year. The process involves logging into eKel, reviewing specialization options, making a selection, and receiving confirmation from the system.

 2.5.4 Activity diagram for View Results

Activity diagram explains how students view their exam results, grades, and academic standing through the Faculty Information System (FIS). The process involves logging into FIS, navigating to the results section, and viewing detailed grade breakdowns including GPA and academic class standing.

 2.5.5 Activity diagram for Upload Grades

Activity diagram explains how academic staff upload student grades into the Faculty Information System (FIS). The process involves staff logging into FIS, manually entering grades for their modules, and submitting the grades to make them available to students.

 2.5.6 Activity diagram for Check Class Eligibility

Activity diagram explains how academic staff and Head of Department manually verify student graduation eligibility and class standing based on degree requirements. The process involves retrieving student records, manually checking GPA and credit requirements, and documenting eligibility status.

 2.5.7 Activity diagram for Get Advisor Guidance

Activity diagram explains how students receive academic guidance from advisors through physical meetings or email communication. The process involves students contacting their advisor, scheduling meetings or email exchanges, and advisors providing personalized guidance based on student records.

 2.5.8 Activity diagram for Get Trends & Stats

Activity diagram explains how the Head of Department manually generates department performance trends and statistics for analysis. The process involves collecting performance data from various sources, manually compiling statistics, calculating trends, and preparing reports for decision-making.

 2.5.9 Activity diagram for Get Module Stats

Activity diagram explains how academic staff manually generate module performance statistics for course analysis. The process involves collecting grade data from FIS, manually calculating pass rates and averages, analyzing performance patterns, and preparing module reports with recommendations for improvement.
